Client Objectives

  • The Digital Operations and IT Innovation group is seeking a highly qualified software development delivery team to assist in building the next generation AoA website​.
  • Their vision is to create a global distributed development platform to deliver a consistent customer-centric digital experience.
  • Their mission is to establish a collaborative methodology to meet Audi Business, Technology and Marketing needs in order to create efficiency & synergy between Audi Development partners across the globe.
  • This Request for Proposal will occur in two distinct phases as outlined below. After Phase 1, AoA will have the discretion to reduce the number of participants to a group of ‘finalists’ that will proceed to Phase 2. ​
  • PHASE 1 – Supplier Capability Assessment & Organizational Approach:
    • Assessment on the technical capabilities in software development, including experience with Adobe Experience Manager, front-end and full-stack engineering.
    • Evaluate automotive experience and strategic approach of digital platforms in an OEM product portfolio.
    • Assess supplier experience in a global distributed development structure​
    • Understand supplier approach and experience in Test Driven (TDD), Behavior Driven Development (BDD) and continuous delivery processes​
  • PHASE 2 - Technical Capability Assessment:
    • Audi will have the ability to ask for Phase 2 based upon Phase 1 submissions. During Phase 2, Audi will ask the supplier ‘finalists’ to conduct either a “Short Form” or “Long Form” assignment related to the activities requested in this RFP.
    • Short Form - Strip the branding (if necessary) from your most relevant project / product code and walk us through the entire codebase and architecture​.
    • Long Form - Execute a development assignment authored and assigned by Audi where the supplier will be given requirements and specifications to develop front-end components (this may include at least lightweight service integrations to existing endpoints). Supplier must develop working & testable code within a specified time period.

02

Scope

The Digital Operations and IT Innovation departments at Audi of America (AoA) is seeking a highly-qualified software development and delivery team to assist in the technical development and coding of our Next Generation Website over the next year (May 2017 – March 2018). As a participant, your organization will have the choice to bid on, and showcase capability for this collaborative development and delivery role of the www.Audiusa.com site redesign. In 2014, Audi of America identified the growing impact of digitalization across the car shopping experience and developed a best-in-class OEM brand site experience for its customers.

As part of the collaboration with Audi AG, we have determined there is a CORE foundation of software that will be managed on a global level and customizable site sections for which Audi of America will enhance for its market needs (and potentially other market use). The *initial* project overview is outlined below (also in Exhibit 4) whereas the Supplier Delivery / Development team will be responsible for the “CUSTOMIZED – AoA Modified “NEMO” Platform” and
“MARKET – AoA-only Development” sections.

Deliverables

Audiusa.com Redesign Development involves, but is not limited to:

​ The planning, development, testing and overall production of the new audiusa.com brand site in collaboration with Audi Brand and CODE (IT) offices under the direction of the Leadership and Solutions & Design teams. Production will be based upon the global Audi AG “NEMO” architecture which includes microservices development, AEM (6.2), use of modern front-end frameworks (FB React, etc.) and leverages automated DevOps supported build and deploy methodologies for development using TDD principles.

  • AGILE methodology and management for the development to be conducted in 1-2 week SPRINT cycles with continuous delivery pipelines as defined & managed by Solutions & Design architects.
  • Project Management and organization of associated meetings between Solutions & Design Team and Delivery teams.
  • SPRINT organization, planning and documentation on performance​.
  • 10 months version with tasks executed in 1 or 2 week SPRINTS (Thurs-Wed sprint cadence).
  • 12 month version with tasks executed in 1 or 2 week SPRINTS (Thurs-Wed sprint cadence)​.
  • Routing for Reviews and Approvals.
  • Allocation of resources to appropriate projects / tasks to meet deadlines.
  • Progress and performance traceability for Solutions & Design teams & Leadership teams.
  • Communications with internal AoA IT group
  • Adherence to AoA IT standards and best practices for integrated development and contributions to AudiUSA.com.

​Quality Assurance planning and oversite of unit tests developed into the codebase of each microservice and front-end component.

  • Development of unit testing implementation into codebase.
  • Validation that code was developed appropriately and sustainably as part of warranty of work.
  • Oversight of QA principles & software for consistency across all developers.
  • Ongoing bug and defect management and backlog.
